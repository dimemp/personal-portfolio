Description

For six years, I led the Greek chapter of Sigma Squared, a global community for the world’s most ambitious founders under 26. In this role, I recruited 10+ high-impact founders and conducted about 100 interviews with international candidates. I also proposed the standardized evaluation scale currently used by the organization worldwide for new members selection. Today, I continue to support the network as a Fellow within a global group of over 1,000+ members.
