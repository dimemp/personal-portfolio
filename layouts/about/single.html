My story

I grew up between my father’s active construction sites and the enduring legacy of my grandfather’s work. Spending my summers in a village defined by the buildings, warehouses, and the local hospital he engineered, I learned early that a successful project is one that stands the test of time. This drive to create led me to study Mechanical Engineering at NTUA, though I soon found that my true passion was for the act of building rather than the weight of theory.

In 2015, I transitioned from physical blueprints to digital designs by developing a web application called Partybuzz, which marked my first significant engagement with the tech startup community. Since then, I have launched four major web applications and created numerous digital products. I have managed over 230,000 lines of code during the entire product lifecycle, including server-side DevOps, MySQL architecture, Laravel backend, JavaScript frontend, and the complete brand identity.

Building products naturally led to building communities. Through Mindspace and the Greek Startup Universe, I’ve supported over 3,000 students and 2,300 startups. These programs have fostered founders who achieved €100M+ in total exits, raised over €15M in VC funding, and created 100+ jobs to date.

This mission culminated in Founderhood, a global ecosystem designed to ensure that talent—no matter where it's located—has access to opportunity. Whether I’m writing the 150,000th line of a SaaS platform or designing a visual identity, my goal remains the same: building high-integrity tools that solve real human problems and stand the test of time.
