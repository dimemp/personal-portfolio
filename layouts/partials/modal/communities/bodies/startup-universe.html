Description

I co-initiated Startup Universe, one of the largest initiatives for tech startups. This program connects hundreds of early-stage startups with the brightest minds in the diaspora, including established founders, renowned investors, talented engineers, academics, and experts, through open online national programs.

The program has reached over 1,500 startups and has engaged more than 2,000 participants across more than 30 countries. Additionally, it has brought together over 170 experts and 70 investors and has facilitated funding deals for several startups.
