Description

I served as an Ambassador in Greece for Greektech, a community originally founded in New York to support the Greek and Cypriot tech ecosystems in entering the U.S. market.

As an ambassador, I focused on high-level strategy and ecosystem connectivity. I acted as a liaison between the organization and the local community, identifying and facilitating connections with potential donors, strategic partners, and high-growth startups.
